Template Introduction

Our templates are power by Bootstrap


1. Choose your text editor

I personally use VSCode but you can use a Text Editor of your choise. You can get VSCode Here.


2. Download Our Template's Files

I currently have only one template for download available. Please feel free to download it and make all the changes you want. It's a simple template. The compresed file has all the images I used for the template. You can find all the images I used Here.


3. Service Section

The template's Service Section has six different icons with hovering effects. You can change these icons as you like.


4. Paralax Section

The Paralax scrolling is one of my favorite effects. Parallax scrolling will show the background content moving at a different speed that the actual foreground content while scrolling.


5. Additional Components

5.1 Google Maps

The Google Section of the Templates allows you to place a map of your choice. I quote Google Developer it self, "The Maps Embed API lets you place an interactive map, or Street View panorama on your web page with a simple HTTP request; no JavaScript required." You can see more of that article Here.


5.2 Social Icons

The Social Media section has at least three main icons. You can change them as you wish. The texting icon has a HTML tag that allows the client to click and contact you directly via text.
