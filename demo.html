Template Introduction

Our templates are power by Bootstrap


1. Text

Once you purchase our template, we will edit the text with your business information and services.


2. Images

We can also change the images. We highly recomend using the images from Unplash. They have plenty of free high-resolution images, with all the sizes and orientation.


3. Service Section

The template's Service Section has six different icons with hovering effects. We can change these icons to icons that better aligns to your business.


4. Paralax Section

The Paralax scrolling is one of our favorite effects. Parallax scrolling will show the background content moving at a different speed that the actual foreground content while scrolling.


5. Additional Components

5.1 Google Maps

The Google Section of the Template allows you to place a map of your store location. As the Google Developer's page says, "The Maps Embed API lets you place an interactive map, or Street View panorama on your web page with a simple HTTP request; no JavaScript required." You can see more of that article Here.


5.2 Social Icons

The Social Media section has at least three main icons. We can change them as you wish. The texting icon has a HTML tag that allows the client to click and contact you directly via text.
